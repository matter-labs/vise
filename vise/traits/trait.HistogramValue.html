Expand description

Value of a Histogram.

This trait is implemented for signed and unsigned integers (i64, u64, isize, usize), f64 and Duration. To use smaller ints and floats as Histogram values, they can be converted to their larger-sized variants (e.g., i16 to i64, u32 to u64, and f32 to f64).
